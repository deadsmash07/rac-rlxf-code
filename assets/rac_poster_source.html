Retroactive Advantage Correction  (RAC)
A reward that arrives Δ steps late still belongs to the rollout that earned it.

Arnav Raj
IIT Delhi
github.com/deadsmash07/rac-rlxf-code
Slow reward channels — code verifiers, judge ensembles, human review — return several gradient steps after the rollout that earned them. RAC queues each pending reward, ages it, and reinjects it as a clipped residual into the optimizer step where it lands. Closed-form, no reward-model retraining, a two-line patch on PPO or GRPO.

The guarantee

Cumulative bias is linear in the kernel slack η — exactly zero when the effective delay kernel reinjects all its mass, and reducing to V-trace at the no-delay identity kernel. A Pinsker / Bretagnolle–Huber TV bound (crossover KL*≈1.6259) keeps each step's policy shift controlled.
